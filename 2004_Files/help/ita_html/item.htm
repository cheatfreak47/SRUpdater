Treasures along the Way...
Course Points of Interest

Rings These are "key" to your success in the game. You need to collect a
different amount per course. How many do you need? Why do you
need them? Telling would be cheating! Maybe you'll just need to
look around and find out for yourself.
Item Panel This red, yellow and blue item will come in handy, so be sure to pick it
up as you run around the course. There are different powers your
character gains for a short time when you run through an Item Panel.
Fleet Feet Fleet Feet increases your character's speed. As if your character wasn't
fast enough already!
Water Shield With a Water Shield (blue sphere), you can walk or run on water!
Keep in mind that you can only use the shield once, so use it wisely!
Lightning Shield When a Lightning Shield (yellow sphere) appears around your character,
there is no need to touth the Rings. Just run by them and the Lightning
Shield will suck them in! Remember that falling in the water makes the
shield disappear.
Big 5 Ring5 rings will be added to your Ring count.
Big 10 Ring10 rings will be added to your Ring count.
Big 20 Ring20 rings will be added to your Ring count.
Ring Gate This gate will be open only if you have enough rings for the number displayed
above it. Through this, you may take a short cut, or may find Chaos Emeralds......
Ring Gate is also "key".
Accelerators Run through an Accelerator and you are propelled forward at an
unbelievable speed. Of course, riding this high-tech wonder machine
will require a fee in Rings. The more rings you have, the further
the Accelerator will send you.
Keep in mind that your rings will run out with Warp System.When you want to
save your rings, you better avoid it.
Sonic Tokens Find the five Sonic Tokens located on each course. Gather all of them,
continue to run, and something rather unexpected might happen.
The search is well worth the effort.
Chaos Emeralds And what would a Sonic game bo without Chaos Emeralds? Race around the
tracks to find the Emeralds, but only the best racers can keep them.

Checking Chaos Emeralds
You will check the Chaos Emeralds your character has collected so far from the LOAD/ SAVE DATA of
the option menu.
