Character Descriptions
     Sonic
Sonic is the fastest of all the racers. He's so fast that he almost lifts off the ground when he runs,
so take care when cornering or you might miss the turn! Sonic has excellent jumping ability too.
JumpingAction Key to jump. In mid-air, press again for a Double Jump.
Direct the jump with the Direction Key.
Spin Dash RollDirection Key DOWN while running for a Spin Dash, then UP (hold) to continue dashing.
Or, press the Direction Key DOWN while standing still to crouch, then Accel Key.

     Tails
Tails is not as fast as Sonic, but he accelerates and corners well.
Jump and grip are fairly good too, but best of all, he can fly!
FlyingAction Key to jump. In mid-air, press again and hold to fly. Direct the flight
with the Direction Key.
Spin Dash RollDirection Key DOWN while running for a Spin Dash, then UP (hold) to
continue dashing. Or, press the Direction Key DOWN while standing still to
crouch, then Accel Key.

     Knuckles
Knuckles is the overall well-balanced contestant in the race. His ability to stay on the road is outstanding,
and cornering is good. Knuckles can glide after jumps.
GlidingAction Key to jump. In mid-air, press again and hold to glide.
Spin Dash RollDirection Key DOWN while running for a Spin Dash, then UP (hold) to continue dashing.
Or, press the Direction Key DOWN while standing still to crouch, then Accel Key.
